Mastering Parent Engagement Plus
ISQ, South Bank
23rd October 2025

Mastering Parent Engagement Plus was a guided community of practice for parent engagement champions who wanted to take the next step in their parent engagement journey. Throughout this year-long program, which followed on from ISQ’s flagship Mastering Parent Engagement program, participants gathered once a term to hear from experts and from each other as they worked on their identified parent engagement goals.

Between Masterclasses, participants were mentored by respected independent school leaders who had successfully implemented parent engagement within their own school communities. By the end of the year, each participant produced an article on their project, which was published by ISQ and the QIS Parents Network.
